About

Jan 8th, 2018

Hey, I’m Thomas, a French software engineer, working for an awesome company called JobTeaser.

You can find me on Github and Linkedin.

I’m a Terminal-fan, Vim-addict kind of guy. I’m also a keyboard enthusiast and use Bépo (a French Dvorak-like variant) as my every day keyboard layout.

The purpose of this blog is to share what I believe is interesting content, be it a Ruby-jedi-trick or a cool app that I can’t live without.

Since English is not my mother tongue, I might happen to make some grammar / spelling mistakes. Deal with it (or leave me a comment somewhere, I’d be glad to correct it).
